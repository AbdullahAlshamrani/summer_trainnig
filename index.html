Arsenal remembers COVID-19 victims

During the past three months, we have received correspondence from a significant number of families and friends of fans who died due to the COVID-19 pandemic. We would like to recognise these supporters with a special tribute in our matchday programme on our ‘home’ return, when we are scheduled to play Norwich City. The club welcomes short obituaries to: programme@arsenal.co.uk. There will hopefully be scope to include a photo so please keep the tribute to no more than 30 words, which we will require no later than Monday, June 15. We will also require an address in order that we can send those concerned a complimentary programme. The thoughts of Arsenal Football Club are with those many supporters who have been affected by this devastating virus.
